Privacy Policy

Muttaqi · Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Muttaqi mobile application ("Muttaqi", "the app", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using Muttaqi you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Who we are

Muttaqi is an Islamic companion app offering Quran reading, Hadith, Duas, prayer times, Qibla direction, a recitation helper, and an AI assistant. For any privacy questions, contact us at muttaqiapp@gmail.com.

2. Using the app without an account (Guest mode)

You can use most of Muttaqi as a guest without creating an account. In guest mode we do not collect your name or email, and your data (such as reading position and settings) stays on your device.

3. Information we collect

a) Account information

When you create an account, we collect:

DataWhy
NameTo personalise the app (e.g. greetings)
Email addressTo create and secure your account, and for password recovery
Password (email sign-up)Handled and encrypted entirely by our third-party authentication provider — we never see or store your password

If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from your Google account. We do not collect your Google profile photo.

b) Information stored in your account (cloud sync)

If you are signed in, the following is stored securely in our cloud database so it can sync across your devices:

  • Profile: name, email, preferred language, and madhab (school of jurisprudence)
  • App settings: prayer-time calculation preferences, notification preferences, theme
  • Your chosen prayer-time city and country (so prayer times work on a new device)
  • Worship streak and daily-challenge progress

c) Location

Prayer times are city-based. You choose your city from a list, and prayer times are calculated on your device from that city. We do not access, collect, or store your device's GPS location for prayer times, and we store only the city and country you select (see 3b) — never GPS coordinates.

The only time the app uses your device's GPS is for the Qibla compass, and only if you tap "Use GPS" on that screen. In that case your location is used momentarily on your device to calculate the Qibla direction and is not stored, transmitted, or shared. The Qibla feature also works without GPS using your selected city.

d) Microphone & audio (Recitation feature)

Important: When you use the Quran recitation feature, the app records audio only while you actively use that feature and sends the recording to a third-party AI provider (through a secure proxy) to transcribe your recitation. The audio is processed to generate the transcription and is not retained by us. We do not record audio in the background.

e) AI Assistant

When you use the AI assistant, the text of your questions and messages is sent to a third-party AI provider (through a secure proxy) to generate a response. To find the most relevant verses, hadith and duas, your question is also used to search our Islamic-text library held by a third-party search service. We send only the message and general Islamic-calendar context — we do not send your personal profile, history, or activity to these providers. Your AI chat history is stored only on your device.

f) Anonymous AI feedback

If you rate an AI answer (👍 / 👎), we store the question, the answer, and your rating to improve quality. This feedback is anonymous — it is stored with no user identity (no name, email, or account ID).

g) Information stored only on your device

  • Your reading position ("Continue Reading") for Quran and Hadith
  • Your AI chat history
  • Downloaded content (translations, recitations, etc.)

This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the app.

6. How we use your information

  • To provide and personalise the app's features
  • To sync your settings and progress across your devices
  • To calculate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • To send the notifications you have enabled
  • To improve the AI assistant (via anonymous feedback)

7. Data retention & deletion

We keep your account data for as long as your account exists. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time:

  • Email muttaqiapp@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account", from the email address linked to your account.
  • We will delete your account data within 30 days of a verified request.

Uninstalling the app removes all data stored locally on your device.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact muttaqiapp@gmail.com.

9. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored using industry-standard third-party cloud providers. While no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

10. Children's privacy

Muttaqi is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above, and significant changes may be announced within the app.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email muttaqiapp@gmail.com.
